Sparse 2D Echocardiograph to 3D Cardiac Volume Reconstruction Cardiac reconstruction Coordinate-based neural implicit reconstruction from sparse 2-3 slice echo input, with differentiable multi-view projection, learnable pose optimization, Fourier positional encodings, and geometric regularization. Result: 3D Dice 0.864 and 3D IoU 0.765 on MITEA-CAP. Neural ODE Forecasting for Glioblastoma Evolution Brain MRI dynamics Neural ODE-based forecasting on multimodal MRI with a hybrid Attention U-Net and temporal embeddings to evolve latent tumor state in continuous time. Presented at CMU BME Forum 2025. Neural Anisotropic Diffusion Network for MRI Denoising Denoising and restoration Unrolled the Perona-Malik denoising PDE into a differentiable model with learned spatially adaptive conduction weights. Result: 24.85 dB PSNR on Br35H, +4.46 dB over classical baselines, with 47% lower Edge MSE. Dual-View Mammography Breast Cancer Prediction Clinical notebook Aligned MLO to CC mammograms using breast-mask centroid matching and affine translation, then fused the views with a ResNet plus cross-attention classifier. Result: 80% binary prediction on CBIS-DDSM and 99.91% reduction in cross-view centroid distance. FluoroGRID Private repo, manuscript underway Multi-modal quad-encoder framework that fuses live 2D fluoroscopy with 3D pre-operative meshes to estimate real-time 3D deformation. Result: 0.5 mm target registration error at 139 fps, with 370,000+ synthetic 3D/2D training pairs.
